Value proposition

Most teams do not fail because they lack tools. They fail because the real operating knowledge lives in conversations, incident notes, and the heads of a few overloaded people.

The problem

Critical workflows depend on invisible context.

When something changes, teams improvise. They search old tickets, ask the same expert again, and rebuild decisions from fragments.

The work gets done, but the system does not learn. Every exception becomes another manual recovery loop.
