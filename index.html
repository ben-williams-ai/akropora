Spatial AI for challenging real world settings

The challenge

From environmental impact assessments to engineering inspection and carbon crediting, high-stakes decisions across countless sectors still depend on costly boots-on-the-ground surveys. This is slow, labour intensive and vulnerable to human error. Worse still, if something was missed the first time, another expensive field visit is needed.

Our solution

The future is spatial intelligence. Where surveyors once had to trudge through the field for days, a single drone can now image an the same area in minutes, while AUV's can reach areas no human could. Akropora brings cutting-edge spatial AI to these datasets, using techniques honed on challenging real-world settings from urban environments to coral reefs.

Our services

What we offer

Akropora works with 2D imagery, 3D models and audio data from challenging real-world settings. If it seems too difficult for AI, then Akropora is here to fix that. We offer:

  • High-resolution, lightweight 3D models that open in a web browser using Gaussian splats.
  • AI integrations including plain-English search across the model and the underlying survey imagery.
  • Generate highly accurate detectors for difficult classes using fast human-guided model tuning.
  • Scale expert input across 2D imagery, 3D data or audio.
  • Get to the details satellite imagery can't, locate the rarest objects in mountains of image data, find acoustic signals across years of audio recordings, and more.
