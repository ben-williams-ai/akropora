Spatial AI for challenging real world settings

The challenge

From environmental impact assessment to engineering inspection and carbon crediting, high-stakes decisions across countless sectors still depend on costly boots-on-the-ground surveys. This is slow, labour intensive and vulnerable to human error. Worse still, if something was missed the first time, another expensive field visit is needed.

Our solution

The futue is spatial intelligence. Where an army of surveyors once trudged through the field, a single drone can now image an entire site in minutes. Akropra brings cutting-edge spatial AI to these datasets using techniques honed on the most challenging real-world settings. Akropora works across 2D, 3D and audio.

Our services

What we offer

Akropora works with 2D imagery, 3D models and audio data from challenging real-world settings. We offer:

  • High-resolution, lightweight 3D models that open in a web browser using Gaussian splats.
  • AI integrations including plain-English search across the model and the underlying survey imagery.
  • Generate highly accurate detectors for difficult classes using fast human-guided model tuning.
  • Scale expert input across 2D imagery, 3D data or audio.
  • Find branching coral on a reef, vegetation below satellite resolution, acoustic calls in a recording set, or the site-specific evidence your survey needs to track.
