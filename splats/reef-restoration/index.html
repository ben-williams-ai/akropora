Click and hold left mouse button to rotate, right mouse button to pan, scroll to zoom, double click to select focal point.

This demo shows a precise spatial twin of a 20x30m area of coral reef in Indonesia restored by Mars (buildingcoral.com). The reef is approximately 3 years post-restoration. This twin provides a tangible digital asset to evidence and explore the restoration progress.

Data courtesy of: J.Stratford, B.Williams, A. Dwiyanto, the Mars Coral Restoration Project Monitoring Team, T.B.Razak, and T.A.C.Lamont.
