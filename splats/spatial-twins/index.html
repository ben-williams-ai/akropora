This is a work in progress. We are adding an improved version soon, with higher detail and the current missing patches filled in.

This demo shows a spatial twin of Jastrzębia Góra in Poland, built from 20k drone images gathered in a single flight. The model spans roughly 3km at its widest point.

Akropora produces high-quality Gaussian splats for large real-world sites, making vast areas viewable directly in the browser.
